flower arrangements

Lauren Flowers is a boutique floral studio in London, UK. Designing with the unconventional in mind, they aim to create meaningful compositions in flowers of unravelling beauty.
Conscious of the emotional intent behind every piece, special importance placed on the movement throughout.

Lauren Flowers' style can be described as sculptural, juxtaposing edgy with feminine.
